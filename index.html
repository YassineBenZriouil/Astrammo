How to Play

Game Overview

Astrammo is an action-packed arcade game where you defend against waves of enemy ships.

Basic Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move your ship
  • Spacebar: Shoot
  • P: Pause game

Game Mechanics

  • Start with 10 ammo and 3 lives
  • Each shot uses 1 ammo
  • Kill enemies to get +10 ammo
  • No ammo = No shooting!
  • Get coins for each enemy killed

Enemy Types

  • Basic Enemy: Moves straight down, 1 hit to destroy
  • Zigzag Enemy: Moves in wave pattern, 2 hits to destroy
  • Kamikaze Enemy: Chases player, 1 hit to destroy
  • Boss Enemy: Large enemy with 10 health, shoots at player

Power-ups

  • Blue Orb: +20 ammo
  • Green Orb: Temporary shield (10 seconds)
  • Red Orb: Rapid fire (5 seconds)

Combo System

  • Build combos by destroying enemies quickly
  • Combo resets after 2 seconds of no hits
  • Higher combos = Higher score multiplier
  • +10% score per combo level

Shop System

  • Use coins to buy new ships
  • Gray Ship: Default ship
  • Blue Ship: 100 coins
  • Red Ship: 200 coins
